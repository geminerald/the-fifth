Gnomes of Red Rock

Status: Unknown

Red Rock is the largest Gnomish city in the region. A technocracy with many guilds they are largely unaffected by the Red Hand thus far. However in an effort to hide their mountain from the ivading army they have inadvertendly sealed the vent for the poison gas which the mountain produces. They require urgent aid to release the trapped mining team and ensure the safety of their people

Tiri Kitor

Status: Occupied

A wild community of elves who live on the edges of the swamps and woods of the Vale. Known as great flyers they have a symbiotic relationship with their mounts and they practice flying from an early age. Recently they have come under attack in their home swamps from Saarvith and his Dragon, along with other unspeakable abominations. They are currently evacuating - only the destruction of the Facility in Rhest and the elimination of all threats in this region can stop them fleeing.

Human Refugees

Status: Allied but Useless

Survivors from Drellin's Ferry and other hamlets in the region have come flocking to Brindol. They are currently living in a tent city outside the walls of Brindol. There are relatively few combatants but those that there are have joined the Brindol militia to fight The non-combatants are mainly seen as a drain on resources in the area and as marauding bands of raiding Tiamat followers infest the region it seems unlikley that they can move any time soon.
